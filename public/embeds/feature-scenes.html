Status · concept board v1

Local ideation only — lives on W:\ for now; the design system stays read-only. Same conventions as the icon, widget and memoji boards: single-family aura gradients (icon-set ramps, radial 35/28), 8px squircles, shared .av chips and aura tiles. Three families only — apricot, rose, lavender — the design system's locked colour architecture (tailwind.preset.js: "apricot → rose → lavender"). No gold: gold is a Finance OS accent, not Health OS. Assignment: comms/scheduling/conversion = rose (signature), CRM/programs/AI/reporting = lavender, payments/automation/content = apricot. Next: pick winners, tighten copy, then export fixed-size SVG/PNG per scene — and optionally animate the winners for video.
