# Brand

A one-page overview of who Health OS is — the strategy the visual and verbal foundations serve. v2 keeps the v1 brand entirely; only the *craft* (type pairing and structural patterns) evolves.

## Mission
Give wellness practitioners one calm operating system to run their business — so structure, automation and client activation stop living in a dozen disconnected tools.

## Vision
Every independent wellness practice runs on Health OS the way a modern company runs on its CRM: quietly, reliably, in the background — leaving the practitioner free to do the work only they can do.

## Values
- **Calm over clever.** The system absorbs complexity so the user doesn't have to.
- **Structure first.** Opinionated defaults beat blank canvases.
- **Show the mechanism.** We explain what's happening behind the scenes, not just the outcome.
- **Warmth with rigour.** Human and caring, but engineered to a high bar.

## Positioning statement
For wellness practitioners drowning in fragmented tools, **Health OS** is a business operating system that unifies scheduling, automation and client activation into one calm surface — unlike point apps that each solve a sliver and leave the practitioner to stitch them together.

## Target audience
Independent and small-team wellness **practitioners** — across coaching practices, therapy and clinical practices, studios and wellness businesses — replacing a patchwork of booking apps, spreadsheets, email tools and reminders. They serve **clients**.

**Persona — "Hayley":** runs a growing practice, excellent at her craft, allergic to admin. She doesn't want another app; she wants the admin to disappear.

## Audience insight
They didn't get into wellness to become operators. Every tool they adopt adds another login, another thing to maintain. What they crave isn't more features — it's the feeling that the back office is *handled*.

## Archetype
**Sage 70% / Creator 30%.** The Sage brings clarity, calm and quiet authority — it has already untangled the mess. The Creator brings the craft and the sense that something well-made is being built around the practitioner.

## Anti-archetype
Not the **Hero** (no hustle, no "crush your goals"), not the **Jester** (no gimmicks), not the **Magician** that hides the mechanism. We don't shout, we don't gamify, we don't oversell.

## Core promise
**Structure + automation + activation** — the three things a wellness business needs to run itself, in one place.

## How v2 expresses the brand
The strategy is unchanged; the surface is the "operating system" made literal:
- **A command-palette hero** — a search bar with ⌘K and `/command` chips — says "this is the surface you run your practice from" before a word of copy.
- **Anonymous Pro (monospace) body** gives every label and paragraph a quiet terminal texture — the OS positioning, felt rather than stated.
- **Spline Sans headings** keep the voice human and warm above that texture.
- **Soft pastel glows on warm ivory** carry the multi-hue brand without flooding it.
- **Dark-carbon pill CTAs and a rounded carbon footer** anchor the calm palette with one confident dark.
- **A gentle marquee of tool cards** shows, calmly, the dozen tools Health OS replaces.

Everything stays flat, warm and premium — zero glass, no 3D — so the feeling in five seconds is *relief, not adrenaline*.
